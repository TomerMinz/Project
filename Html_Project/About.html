About Timinz Theatre

Welcome to Timinz Theatre, where the magic of live performance comes alive! Our theatre is dedicated to bringing you the best in entertainment, from classic plays to modern musicals.

Our History

Timinz Theatre was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate theatre enthusiasts who wanted to create a space where people could come together to experience the thrill of live theatre. Since then, we have grown into one of the most beloved theatres in the city, known for our high-quality productions and intimate setting.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and entertain our audiences through the power of live theatre. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where people of all ages and backgrounds can come together to experience the magic of theatre.
